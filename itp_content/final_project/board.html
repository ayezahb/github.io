Raemier Javelosa

Founder and President

Raemier is currently attending the University of Notre Dame in Notre Dame, Indiana for a major in Biological Sciences with a minor in Compassionate Care in Medicine. She graduated from the Academy of Our Lady of Guam back in 2020.

She started this organization back in 2021 to help Guam high school students become competitive applicants for off-island colleges and universities.
